Services

* Free sample of 10 photo scans, no obligation

Photo scan

$.15 ea

This includes 4x6, 5x7, or similar sizes. Scans are done at 300 or 600 dpi. Backside will be scanned for no extra charge if there is writing on it.

Slide, film, or negative scan

$.40 ea

35 mm projector slides, as well as film and/or negatives sized at 110, 126, and 135. Scans are done at 13 MP or 22 MP.

XL pic/scrapbook page scan

$.50 ea

This is anything bigger than a standard piece of paper or a scrapbook page as they require a different scanner, price is per side. 300 or 600 dpi.

VHS digitization

$25 ea

VHS of any length up to 6 hours. MP4 720x480 resolution.

Prices include basic touch up such as red eye removal, color restoration, getting rid of creases, etc.

About

Hi my name is Garrett, I live in Salt Lake City UT. After digitizing my family's pictures and videos, I realized how great it was to have the memories safe and accessible 24/7 on my phone. This inspired me to provide this as a service for others. Popular digitization businesses require sending your family memories accross the country and trusting that the mail system will get it there safely, and that they will be returned. I work locally in the Salt Lake Valley and can come to you for pickup, or you can drop them off at my place of business. Most orders are usually done within a few weeks. I put the pics/videos either on a flash drive for an extra $10 or I can upload them to a cloud service of your choice. If you have any questions please reach out.
